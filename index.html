CAREER PLANS

PEDIATRICIAN

I want to become a pediatrician. I love working with kids; they have such pure hearts, and it is always exciting to be with them. They bring out the goodness in you and make you forget about any other life problems you might have. After my brother had his two surgeries before the age of one, I knew at that moment that I wanted to contribute to my community by giving back to children and helping them in any way I can.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Liam Brown

What do I want to be? I aspire to pursue a career in the health profession as a pediatrician. I have a passion for working with children and am committed to promoting their well-being. My goal is to support their growth and development by ensuring they maintain optimal health.

How did this idea came to mind: From a young age, I aspired to become a medical professional. During high school, I enrolled in a Career and Technical Education (CTE) course that introduced me to coding and programming, focusing on website development. I enjoyed this field and believed it was the direction I would pursue. However, by the summer of 2024, I realized that software engineering was not my true calling. Conversations with various individuals and the fulfilling experience of assisting others sparked a desire within me to make a meaningful impact.

My fondness for children has always been significant, and taking care of my brother and cousins deepened my affection for young ones. A pivotal moment for me came when reflecting on my brother's early health challenges; he underwent two surgeries by the age of eight months, and our family spent a considerable amount of time in the hospital during that first year. This experience motivated me to focus on helping young children and ensuring their well-being.

The compassion shown to my brother by his pediatrician has inspired me to become that source of support for others, motivating me to strive for excellence in the medical field.

HOW DO I PLAN ON ACHIVING THIS GOAL?

To become a pediatrician, I first need to obtain my bachelor's degree in pre-med or biology, then attend medical school and undergo three years of training, totaling approximately 11 to 13 years of education and training.
